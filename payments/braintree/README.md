# Braintree

[Braintree](https://www.braintreepayments.com/) is a full-stack payments platform that makes it easy to accept payments in your app or website. It is available for merchants in the United States, Canada, Australia, Europe, Singapore, Hong Kong SAR China, Malaysia, and New Zealand. Merchants can use Braintree to accept **PayPal**, **Apple Pay**, **Google Pay**, **Venmo**, and most credit and debit cards, including **Visa**, **Mastercard**, **American Express**, **Discover**, **JCB**, and **Dinerâ€™s Club**.

Commerce Layer provides out-of-the-box **server-side** integration with Braintree. This section will walk you through the different steps you need to follow in order to let your customers process their payments using the Braintree payment gateway on our platform.
